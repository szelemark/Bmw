About the M4 GTS

The BMW M4 GTS is a limited-edition, high-performance coupe engineered for the track but legal for the road. It features a water-injection system for increased power, lightweight carbon fiber components, and a motorsport-inspired suspension. The aggressive aerodynamics, including a manually adjustable rear wing and front splitter, provide exceptional downforce and stability at high speeds.

Inside, the M4 GTS offers a focused, driver-centric cockpit with Alcantara accents, racing seats, and a roll cage. Despite its track focus, it retains essential comforts and advanced BMW technology, making it a thrilling yet practical choice for enthusiasts.
